ABOUT THE GAME

Thirty years after Freddy Fazbear's Pizza closed its doors, the events that once terrified children have faded into urban legend. Now, a new horror attraction has been built from the remains — salvaged props, broken animatronics, security footage, and memories no one wanted to remember. The owners claim it’s all staged. They were wrong.
